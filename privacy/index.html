Timbrelis Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

Developer: Alta1r Dev

Privacy contact: nauvalwidiatma@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Timbrelis accesses, uses, stores, and shares data. Timbrelis is an Android app for realtime audio observation, visualization, research, and hobby use.

Summary

The current version of Timbrelis processes microphone audio locally on the device. The current version does not record audio, save audio files, upload audio, use accounts, show ads, or use analytics SDKs.

If ads, analytics, cloud features, accounts, crash reporting, or any network-based data processing are added in a future release, this Privacy Policy, the in-app privacy notice, and the Google Play Data safety disclosure must be updated before that release is published.

Microphone Access

Timbrelis requests access to the device microphone when the user enables microphone-based audio visualization.

Microphone audio is used to calculate realtime visual and signal-analysis features inside the app. Audio frames are processed locally in device memory while the microphone feature is active.

The current version does not record microphone audio as audio files, save microphone audio to device storage, upload microphone audio to a server, or share microphone audio with third parties.

Data Collected or Shared

The current version of Timbrelis does not intentionally collect, transmit, or share personal or sensitive user data.

The current version does not intentionally collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, location, photos, videos, files, payment information, account identifiers, advertising identifiers, app activity for analytics, or crash logs through a third-party SDK.

Ads and Analytics

The current version does not include ads or analytics SDKs.

Google ads or analytics may be considered for a future release. If those features are added, this policy will be updated to list the SDKs used, the data collected, the purpose of collection, sharing practices, retention, and user choices. The Google Play Data safety form must also be updated to match the released app.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because the current version does not store account data or upload user data to a server, Alta1r Dev does not retain server-side user data for Timbrelis.

Users can revoke microphone permission in Android settings or uninstall the app to remove app data managed locally by Android.

Data Security

The current version processes audio locally on the device and does not transmit microphone audio to a server.

Children

Timbrelis is not directed to children. The app is intended for general audio observation, research, and hobby use.

Third-Party Services

The current version does not use third-party ads, analytics, account, or cloud-processing services.

The app uses third-party open-source software components. Those components are listed in the Open Source Licenses notice and do not make Timbrelis open source.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Alta1r Dev at nauvalwidiatma@gmail.com.
