Timbrelis Closed Testing

Developer: Alta1r Dev

Contact: nauvalwidiatma@gmail.com

Purpose

This page describes the limited closed-test build of Timbrelis. The test is intended to check microphone permission flow, realtime visualization, basic overlays, stability, readability, and device compatibility before wider release.

Tester Build Limits

  • Recording and export are disabled in this closed-test build.
  • Only basic overlays are exposed: Pitch Timeline, Power, Burst Timeline, and Timbre Radar.
  • Advanced analysis and reporting features may be added later after stability and policy checks.
  • The app does not upload audio, use accounts, show ads, or use analytics SDKs in this build.

Tester Notes

  • Do not redistribute the app package, screenshots, videos, or feature details publicly without permission.
  • Report freezes, crashes, unclear labels, unreadable overlays, permission problems, and unusual audio response.
  • Results are exploratory and depend on device microphone quality, source distance, background noise, and device settings.
